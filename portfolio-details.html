Understanding Player's Preferences Using Reddit Data

Project information

  • Category: Analytics
  • Project URL: https://github.com/mychetan/understanding_player_preference_using_reddit_data

Using the Playstation 4 (PS4) and Xbox One subreddits, I performed a Natural Language Processing (NLP) in conjunction with a Logistics Regression to capture popular trends observed from each groups. Results observed were then utilized to provide indie game developers suggestions that will help strategize the marketing and development phase of their game.
